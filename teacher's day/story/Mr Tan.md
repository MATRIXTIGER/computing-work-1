# A Story on Mr Tan

Earlier on, I was called to the Principal's office for my misdeed. All morning I had been on tenterhooks in anticipation of telling Mr Tan my error. Propelled by last-minute nerves, I paused at the staircase and stepped into the corridor instead, checking for the last time that I had my shirt tucked in.
